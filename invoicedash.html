Audit Cube: Invoice Dashboard

The Invoice Dashboard in Audit Cube provides users with the tools to manage and review invoices related to shipments. It enables users to track payments, review discrepancies, and export invoice data for further analysis.

Invoice Table Overview

  • Invoice Number: A unique identifier for each invoice.
  • Invoice Date: The date when the invoice was generated.
  • Invoice Status: Displays whether the invoice is paid, pending, or disputed.
  • Total Amount: The total amount due for each invoice.

Invoice Details (Paper Icon)

The blue paper icon next to each invoice opens the detailed invoice page. Users can view:

  • Line items associated with the invoice
  • Invoice adjustments
  • Taxes and additional fees

Manual Invoice Approval and Rejection

  • Approve/Reject Buttons: Located next to each invoice, the green approve button and red reject button allow users to manually approve or reject an invoice.
  • Raising Disputes: If an invoice is rejected, users can raise a dispute by providing details and attaching supporting documents.
  • Audit Trail: The audit trail for each invoice shows a history of changes, providing transparency and traceability for invoice processing.

Invoice Upload

Users can upload invoices manually by clicking the upload button. This feature supports bulk uploads, allowing users to add multiple invoices at once using an Excel template.

Troubleshooting

Invoices Not Loading:

Issue: If the invoices are not loading on the dashboard, it could be due to technical issues or filter misconfigurations.

Solution:

  • Check Filters: Make sure you haven't set date or status filters incorrectly.
  • Refresh the Page: Try refreshing the page.
  • Check Browser: Ensure your browser is up-to-date.
